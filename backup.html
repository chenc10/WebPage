Short Biography

Currently, Caleb Chen Cao is a Ph.D. student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. He is working on Data-driven Crowdsourced Human Computation and supervised by Prof. Lei Chen.

Before joining in graduate school, Caleb received his bachelor's degree in Telecommunication Engineering from School of Information and Communication at Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications in July, 2010. He finished his undergraduate thesis in Daedalus S.A.& Universidad Politécnica de Madrid under Prof. José C. González's supervision.
Research Interests

Data-driven Crowdsourced Human Computation

  • I focus on applications driven by massive and unstructured data
  • The human workforces are enrolled via online approaches as a crowd
  •  Human, who is error-prone, greedy, procrastinated and short-sighted, enables the system
  • Input, output and algorithms
