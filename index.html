Short Biography

Currently, Cheney is a Ph.D candidate student in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology. He is working on Network and supervised by Prof. Bo Li.

Before joining in graduate school, Cheney received his bachelor's degree from Department of Automation at Tsinghua University in July, 2014.
